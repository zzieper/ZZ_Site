About Me

I'm a Senior Mechanical Engineer with deep expertise in opto-mechanical design, precision engineering, and robotics. My career spans the full engineering lifecycle — from initial concept and analysis through fabrication, integration, and field deployment.

I've contributed to programs in defense, space systems, biomedical devices, and autonomous platforms. I thrive on solving technically demanding problems in multi-disciplinary teams where mechanical design must interface tightly with optics, electronics, and software.

Outside of engineering, I'm an avid mechanical puzzle designer and solver — participating in the International Puzzle Party (IPP) where I design and exchange original puzzles with makers worldwide.
